LOKR

Low-Rank Kronecker Product adaptation.

Formula: ΔW = kron(W1, W2) × scale

Factorizes the weight dimensions into smaller factor pairs, then uses the Kronecker product to reconstruct the full-rank structure. Self-adapts between full matrix and low-rank decomposition based on network_dim vs dimension size.

Key options: decompose_both=true (decompose both W1 and W2), lokr_factor=-1 (auto factorization).
