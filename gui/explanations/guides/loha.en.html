LOHA

Low-Rank Hadamard Product adaptation.

Formula: ΔW = (A@B) ⊙ (C@D) × scale

The Hadamard (element-wise) product of two low-rank matrices gives an effective rank of dim² with only 2×dim×(M+N) parameters — significantly more expressive than standard LoRA at the same parameter budget.

Tip: Use network_dim=8 (effective rank 64) as a starting point. Increase to 16 for higher capacity.
