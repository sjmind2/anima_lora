Preprocessing

Two artifact lanes feed training:

1. Caching — resizes images to constant-token buckets and tokenizes captions through Qwen3 + T5, writing {stem}_anima.npz latents and {stem}_anima_te.safetensors sidecars. When shuffle variants > 0, each TE cache holds N captions and the dataloader samples one per training step (turn on use_shuffled_caption_variants in your method config to use them).

2. Masking — runs SAM3 (natural-language text-bubble prompts) and MIT (manga-specific glyph segmenter) in parallel through a tempdir, then merges them into post_image_dataset/masks/<rel>/{stem}_mask.png. Per-tool intermediates are not persisted — the tempdir is cleaned up after merge. When the mask dir exists, training subsets pick it up automatically (see masked_loss in the method config).
